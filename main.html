Anki to written notes

Generate notes from Anki decks.

How to

Export your Anki cards with File -> Export. Choose the Notes in simple text (.txt) format. You can edit them or copy the whole text file into the textbox above. Cards need to be separated with tabs. It doesn't recognise labels, so it's better to export cards without them.

You can substitute tabs with eight spaces.
For example: "Question?        Answer".
You can add a section title by preceding it with a hash(#) symbol. Don't forget to separate it from the rest of the text with a tab, or eight spaces.

How to

Export your Anki cards with File -> Export. Choose the Notes in simple text (.txt) format.
You can edit them or copy the whole text file into the textbox below. Cards need to be separated with tabs. Labels won't be recognised.

See images
See video tutorial

You can substitute tabs with eight spaces.
Example: "Question?        Answer".

You can add a section title by preceding it with a hash(#) symbol. Don't forget to separate it from the rest of the text with a tab, or eight spaces.
